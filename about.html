The Concept

The aim of Funny Cloud is to compare the shapes or silhouttes seen in the clouds between MobilNet and the user. He uploads an image and can see MobilNet prediction. He can also write down whaht he sees and share the prediction and its from by saving the image. The saved images can be seen with the MobilNet and user prediction.
